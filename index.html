Why is it good to walk?

Do you know that walking can improve or maintain your overall health in many ways? For example just 30 minutes walk per day can reduce body fat, strengthen your bones, increase cardiovascular fitness, and boost muscle power and endurance. It can prevent from different types of diseases also.

Walking doesn't require any equipment, it is cheap way to fitness. Also if you join a group of people to walk togehter, you can make it enjoyable and fun part of your life. It is great way of socializing!
